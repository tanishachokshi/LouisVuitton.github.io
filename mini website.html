L.V. THE TRUTH

Louis Vuitton is a French luxury fashion house and brand founded in 1854 by Louis Vuitton.
The Fondation Louis Vuitton grounds its committment to the contemporary arts within the historical perspective.
The LVMH Group and its companies opened a new chapter of patronage with the creation of the fondation.
The building itself was inaugrated on 24th October 2014, the result of nearly 25 years of committment
to the arts, culture and heritage.
The brand is famous for its clothing collection, Fashionable bags, Perfumes and classy footwear.
Louis Vuitton NEVERFULL is the most successfull handbag introduced in 2007.
